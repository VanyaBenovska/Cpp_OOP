Task 7 - Miners
You are given a sequence of strings, each on a new line. 
Every odd line on the console is representing a resource (e.g. Gold, Silver, Copper, and so on), and every even – quantity. 
Your task is to collect the resources and print them each on a new line. 
Print the resources and their quantities in format:
{resource} –> {quantity}
The quantities inputs will be in the range [1 … 2 000]

Examples

Input1:	
Gold
155
Silver
10
Copper
17
stop	

Output:
Gold -> 155
Silver -> 10
Copper -> 17		

Input2:
gold
155
silver
10
copper
17
gold
15
stop	

Output:
gold -> 170
silver -> 10
copper -> 17
